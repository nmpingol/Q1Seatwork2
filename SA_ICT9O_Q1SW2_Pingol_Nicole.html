Characters of Noli Me Tangere

Image Character Description
Crisostomo Ibarra Crisostomo Ibarra is the main character of the story. The priests in high positions dislike him because of his father and his education.
Maria Clara She is loved by everyone because of her looks and nice attitude. She is the lover of Ibarra.
Don Rafael Ibarra He is the father of Crisostomo Ibarra. While his son was studying abroad, he was tortured by the Spaniards in power.
Father/Padre Damaso Damaso is a character who abuses his power as a priest. He is the biological father of Maria Clara.
Captain/Kapitan Tiago He was thought to be the father of Maria Clara. He has many connections in the town.
